Dynamic tool-pool swap benchmark

Voice agents can't hold 50 tools in one session without accuracy collapsing. The swap architecture solves this: a small core kit is always loaded (~6 tools), and a swappable pool (~17 tools) is loaded on demand when the user switches context. The benchmark tests whether the model can (a) call switch_toolset correctly when asked, and (b) immediately use a tool from the newly-loaded pool. 11 turns across 6 scenarios. Tested on both OpenAI Realtime and Gemini Live.
